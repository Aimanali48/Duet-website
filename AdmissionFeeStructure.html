FEE AND OTHER MISCELLANEOUS CHARGES REGULATIONS, 2019

  • COMMENCEMENT:

    • The regulations shall be implemented on Batch-2020 and onwards.

     

    1. APPLICATIONS:

    • The regulations shall apply to all students admitted for BE / B.Arch Program of the University.

     

    1. DEFINITIONS:

    (1) In these regulations, unless the context otherwise requires, the following expressions shall have the meanings hereby, respectively, assigned to them as under:

     

    • “University” means the Dawood University of Engineering and Technology, Karachi.
    • “Act” means the Dawood University of Engineering and Technology, Karachi Act, 2013.
    • “Student” means a student (local and foreign) admitted in the University in accordance with the prescribed procedure against a clear seat.
    • “Fees” means the amount required to be deposited by a local and foreign student as admission fees and semester fees.
    • “Refundable fees” means the amount required to be returned to a student on account of University caution money deposit, excess payment, payment made by error, double payment to the University.
    • “Non-refundable fees” means the Registration Fees.

     

    1. FEE FOR LOCAL STUDENTS (PAYABLE AT THE TIME OF ADMISSION ONLY) (ALL SCHEMES AND CATEGORIES):

    Local Students Foreign Students

    • Admission Fee (Once) 10,000
    • Enrolment Fees (Once) 3,000
    • Documents Verification Fees (Once) 2,500
    • Identity Card Fees (Once) 500

    TOTAL Rs. 16,000/- USD500/-

    • Semester Fee:

    Local Students Foreign Students

    • Tuition Fees (Per Semester) 7,000
    • Games Fees (Per Semester) 1,000
    • Developmental Charges (Per Semester) 2,000
    • Transport Fees (Per Semester) 2,000
    • Examination Fees (Per Semester) 2,000
    • Laboratory / Studio Fees (Per Semester) 1,000
    • Library Membership Fees (Per Semester) 1,000

     

    TOTAL Rs. 16,000/- USD250/-

    1. FEE STRUCTURE FOR SELF FINANCE LOCAL (PAYABLE AT THE TIME OF ADMISSION ONLY)

     

    • Departments Fees:

     

    1. Department of Electronic Engineering 750,000/-
    2. Department of Chemical Engineering 750,000/-
    3. Department of Computer System Engineering 750,000/-

    • Departments Fees:

     

    1. Department of Industrial Engineering and Management 625,000/-
    2. Department of Telecommunication Engineering 625,000/-
    3. Department of Petroleum and Gas Engineering 625,000/-
    4. Department of Architecture and Planning 625,000/-

     

    • Departments Fees:

     

    1. Department of Metallurgy and Materials Engineering 400,000/-
    2. Department of Energy and Environment Engineering 400,000/-

     

    1. Above mentioned amount will be refunded, if candidate is not admitted/ secured admission under the self-finance scheme.
    2. Students admitted under self-finance scheme are also required to make payment of University fees and other miscellaneous charges
    3. University will not refund fees including the self-finance scheme fee after the commencement of classes under any circumstances as per HEC policy.

    3. University will not refunded the self-finance fees scheme after the commencement of classes as per HEC policy.

    These regulations are subject to modification by the competent authorities of the University as may be felt appropriate from time to time.
