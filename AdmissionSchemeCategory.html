    • Description of Categories

    Admission against all following categories is subject to the eligibility of the candidates as laid down in the chapter 4 of this prospectus.

    • No. 01 (Sindh-Urban)
    • HSC: Candidates domiciled in Sindh (Urban) and have passed their S.S.C. and H.SC examinations from any board in Sindh or colleges of Sindh affiliated with Aga Khan University Examinations Board or colleges of Sindh affiliated with Federal Board or an equivalent Foreign Examination from colleges of Sindh.

    • Diploma: Candidates domiciled in Sindh (Urban) and have passed their S.S.C from any board of Sindh and DAE three year diploma from a recognized Board of Technical Education from all over Sindh.
    • No. 02 (Sindh-Rural):
    • HSC: Candidates domiciled in Sindh (Rural) and have passed their S.S.C. and H.SC examinations from any board in Sindh or colleges of Sindh affiliated with Aga Khan University Examinations Board or colleges of Sindh affiliated with Federal Board or an equivalent Foreign Examination from colleges of Sindh.

    • Diploma: Candidates domiciled in Sindh (Rural) and have passed their S.S.C from any board of Sindh and DAE three year diploma from a recognized Board of Technical Education from all over Sindh.

    • No. 03. (Punjab):
    • Candidates domiciled in Punjab Province, nominated by the UET Lahore through Higher Education Department, Govt. of Punjab
    • Candidates domiciled in Punjab Province, nominated by the UET Taxilla through Higher Education Department, Govt. of Punjab
    • Nominees of Government of Punjab through Higher Education Department, Govt. of Punjab.

    • No. 04. (Khybar Pakhtoonkhwa–KPK):
    • Candidates domiciled in Khyber Pakhtoon Khowah Province, nominated by NWFP UET Peshawar through Higher Education Commission, Archive and Library Department, Govt. of Khybar Pakhtoonkhwa.
    • Candidate domiciled in Khyber Pakhtoon Khowah Province, nominated by Higher Education Commission, Archive and Library Department, Govt. of Khybar Pakhtoonkhwa.

    • No. 05. (Balochistan Nominee).

    Nominees from Balochistan whose names are forwarded by the Director Colleges and Higher Education Department, Government of Balochistan, Quetta.

    • No. 06. (Azad Jammu Kashmir-AJK).

    Candidates domiciled in Azad Jammu Kashmir-AJK. District-wise allocation of ten (10) seats for Azad Jammu and Kashmir (AJK) are as under

    • District Muzaffarabad/Neelum/Hatianbala 03 seats
    • District Poonch/Sadnuti 02 seats
    • District Bagh 01 seat
    • District Mirpur/Bhimber 02 seats
    • District Kotli 02 seats

    Total 10 seats

    • No. 07. (FATA Nominee).

    Candidates domiciled in Federally Administered Tribal Areas (FATA) whose names are forwarded by State & Frontier Region Division Government of Pakistan, Islamabad.

    • No. 08. (Gilgit Baltistan).

    Candidates domiciled in Gilgit Baltistan, whose names are forwarded by the Director Education (Colleges)/ Secretary Nomination Board, Government of Gilgit Baltistan, Directorate of Education, Gilgit.

    • No. 9. (OIC Nominee)

    Candidates from OIC whose names are forwarded by the Ministry of Finance Planning & Provincial Coordination (Economic Affairs Division), Government of Pakistan, Islamabad.

    • No. 10. Muslim Districts of Candy (Srilanka) PTAP

    Candidates from muslim districts of Candy Srilanka whose names are forwarded by the Ministry of Finance Planning & Provincial Coordination (Economic Affairs Division), Government of Pakistan, Islamabad.

    • No. 11. Dawood Foundation

    Candidates whose names are forwarded by Dawood Foundation and who have cleared DUET pre-admission Test.

    • No. 12 (Federal Capital Area Nominee).

    Candidates from the Federal Capital Area whose names are forwarded by the Cabinet Secretariat, CAD Government of Pakistan, Islamabad.

    • No. 13. (DUET Teaching Staff Son/ Daughter/ Brother/ Sister)

    Real son/ Real daughter/ Real brother/ Real sister of the university teacher (serving or retired, deceased, on lien or working on deputation with other Institutions) are eligible for admission. First preference will be given to real sons/daughters of university teacher.

    • No.14 (DUET Non-Teaching Staff Son/ Daughter/ Brother/ Sister)

    Real son/ Real daughter/ Real brother/ Real sister of the university employee (other than teacher) (serving or retired, deceased, on lien or working on deputation with other Institutions) are eligible for admission. First preference will be given to real sons/daughters of university employee

    • No.15. (Professional Engineer Sons/ Daughters)

    Real Sons/ Daughters of Professional Engineers have passed HSC (Pre-Engineering) examinations.

    • No.16. (Professional Architect Sons/ Daughters)

    Real Sons/ Daughters of Professional Architects and have passed HSC (Pre- Engineering) examinations.

    • No.17.( GHQ Nominee)

    Children of Army Personnel and whose names are forwarded by Welfare Rehabilitation Directorate, GHQ, Rawalpindi.

    • No.18 (Self Finance)

    • Local

    The Candidates holding the domicile and PRC of any area of Pakistan having passed H.S.C. (Pre-Engineering) Examinations from any authorized Board of Education in Pakistan or an equivalent Foreign Examination recognized as such by the University.

    • Overseas Pakistani / Foreign Candidates
    • Foreign national candidates desiring to apply against this category must have passed H.Sc Pre-Engineering examinations or an equivalent foreign examination with at least 60% aggregate marks.
    • Eligible candidates interested to apply under this category must apply for admission through their Embassies via the Higher Education Commission, Islamabad/ Ministry of Finance Planning & provincial Coordination (Economic Affairs Division). Government of Pakistan, Islamabad.
    • The admission shall be granted only to those applicants who are declared eligible and got equivalent certificate from the Chairman Inter Board Committee Islamabad.
